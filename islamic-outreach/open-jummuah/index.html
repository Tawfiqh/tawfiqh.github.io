Open Jummuah

29 Nov 2017

  • Print out some informational booklets. Namely a “how to pray guide” so that guests understand what is going on when everyone else is performing salaat.

  • If the ISoc is doing 2 jummuahs that week, best to only make one of them the open jummuah and really focus on that jummuah being catered to non-muslims and explaining Islamic terms that might otherwise be incomprehensible. This also means there is a larger group of non-muslims that attend and people will see that there is genuine interest.

  • Normally combined with a bake sale.
  • Pick a good khateeb and a relevant topic for him to speak about.
