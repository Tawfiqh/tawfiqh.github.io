Open Jummuah

29 Nov 2017

-Print out some informational booklets for non-muslims. Namely a “how to pray guide” so that they understand what is going on when everyone else is actually performing salaat. -If the ISoc is doing 2 jummuahs that week, best to only make one of them the open jummuah and really focus on that jummuah being catered to non-muslims. This also means there is a larger group of non-muslims that attend and people will see that there is genuine interest. -Normally combined with a bake sale. -Pick a good khateeb and a good topic for him to speak about.
