The Ansari Blueprints - The blueprints to build your community.

The Ansari Blueprints highlights interesting events, fruitful projects and success stories from the Muslim community.

We interview people in their communities to draw up and publish the blueprints to help others replicate this goodness and create value in their communities too.

We find people who have put on beneficial events in their community and now want to see that goodness spread further afield. We interview them and draw up the blueprints to help others recreate the good work.
