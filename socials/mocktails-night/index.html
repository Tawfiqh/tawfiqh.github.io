Mocktails Night

29 Nov 2017

Book a room (preferably with kitchens/bars)

Get misc fun things from poundland/celebrations to jazz up the room (preferably with a theme - streamers, balloons, fairy lights)

Buy food from tesco: drinks, chips and dips, nice cupcakes, sweets, chocolate (this is better in bulk from poundland), fruit (make sure you have cocktail sticks) - should come to about Â£30

Nice plates and glasses are all in the prayer room from previous times

Get there about 1 hour early to set up the room (take scissors, tape and a knife)

Make sure you have serving dishes/plates for the table

Speakers (and preferably spotify/other decent playlist)

Make sugar syrup for mocktails in advance (granulated sugar + water)
