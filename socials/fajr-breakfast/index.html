Fajr Breakfast

29 Nov 2017

-Meet in the prayer room for fajr. -Tell people to meet at 7, wait until 7:10 for late comers. -Keep people updated on facebook as to where you go for breakfast. That way if they don’t make fajr in the prayer room they can still join for breakfast iA -Then go to a nearby college and cook breakfast, eat and be merry. Either goto the supermarket the night before or wait for the morning and buy food once you see how many people come for fajr. (Need to check opening hours of shops before hand) -Or eat at a nearby cafe that is open at that time. (Need to check opening hours beforehand) -Or eat in hall at a nearby college’s breakfast. (opening times permitting) -If fajr is early, spend time waiting for shops/cafes to open, either do work, zhikr, read Quran, chat, clean the store-cupboard, be merry etc.
